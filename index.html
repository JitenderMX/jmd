About Us

Distributors of Ayurvedic body care.

JMD Group is a pioneering force in the distribution of organic, natural, and Ayurveda-based wellness products. Established over 25 years ago, our journey began with a vision to bring the world's most trusted health and wellness brands to consumers globally.

Our commitment to quality and authenticity drives us to meticulously select products that embody wellness, sustainability, and ethical sourcing. With a foundation built on trust and innovation, we continue to expand our reach, providing access to premium wellness products across 27 markets worldwide.
